Roll-out | Vegan Cinnamon Rolls

Roll out!

Enjoy the smell of Cinnabon without the unhealthy cholesterol ! Here is a great recipe for VEGAN cinnamon rolls.

Ingredients:

Dough- 1 Cup white all-purpose flour, ¼ tsp salt ,2 tbsp. white sugar, 1 packet of dry yeast, 1 tsp. Coconut oil, ¼ cup water

Cinnamon Spread- 2 table spoons of coconut oil, half a cup of cinnamon, 1/4 a cup of brown sugar, a pinch of salt

Procedure:

  1. Once dough mixture is complete, you have to let it rise for 4 hours. 
  2. Roll the dough out
  3. Spread the Cinnamon Spread all over the dough like you’re painting a wall
  4. After, roll it 
  5. Once rolled, cut into sections
  6. Put these sections which now are yourcinnamon rolls, onto a wax sheet in a pan
  7. Put the pan and bake it in the oven
    1. Oven should be 375 degrees
  8. You should bake these cinnamon rolls until they’re golden brown (at least 45 minutes)
