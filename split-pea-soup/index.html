Split Pea Soup

Green soup

It’s been so cold outside lately! Whenever I walk outside, my whole body turns into an icicle. I am totally a spring/summer kind of person, and I am proud of myself for even attempting to step outside into the cold.I made this soup because I needed to warm up my insides. Here is how you make it.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 celery stalk
  • 2 carrots
  • A bag of split peas
  • Olive oil
  • Black pepper

Directions

  1. Preparing the water: 
    • Boil split peas in water with a teaspoon of sea salt.
  2. Preparing the veggies:
    • Cut two carrots

Chop One celery stalk

Mince two cloves of garlic

  1. Adding all the ingredients into a pot
    • In a  separate pot, put 1 tablespoon of olive oil on medium heat. Then Add celery and carrots which you then will  sauté for 5 minutes. Then add garlic and half teaspoon red chili powder and 2 teaspoons of sea salt saute for 1 minute.Add cooked split pea mix and cook for 5 minutes. Lastly, add 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
