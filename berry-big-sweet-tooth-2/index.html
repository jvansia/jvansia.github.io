Berry Big Sweet Tooth Smoothie

Sweet can be good for you

I hope everyone had a healthy and happy new year! I cannot wait to make the best out of 2015. This recipe is for those who have a sweet tooth, but want to stay away from refined sugars. For me, I went through a period where I was feeling defeated by the vegan options I was presented, and I would find the loopholes in being vegan. PETA had a list of junk food that was also vegan, it was called,”Accidentally Vegan Food List”, and it included Hershey’s Chocolate Syrup, Cinnamon Poptarts, English Muffins, etc. (YUCK).I realized that this was not the way I wanted to go about living a vegan lifestyle; I think I was eating worse than before!

This recipe has no refined sugars, and can be made in literally 2 minutes. (And it tastes really, really good!)

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole banana
  • Frozen berry mix
  • Orange juice

Directions:

1.Prepare the ingredients for mixture:

  • Peel 1 banana and place into a VitaMix (any blender is fine, but I prefer this one)
  • Take 3 handfuls of frozen berries and place into Vitamix
  • Pour half a glass of orange juice into Vitamix

2. Mix! :

I put my Vitamix on 6, and I started to blend. Blend it 30 seconds, or whenever you find the consistency that you desire.
