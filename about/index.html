About

My name is Janki, and I am currently a freshman at a small liberal arts college in PA. My school is so small that I know that I am the only vegan in my grade! I had gotten into veganism in high school when my dad introduced it to me. Let me tell you, it was life-changing. My dad, brother, and I all suffered from cystic acne. Since becoming a vegan, the amount of acne we had, went down. It was like magic. At first, I was resistant to give up cheese, but then seeing how my mood and body was changing, it was only fitting that I switched to a vegan lifestyle.

Other than searching, creating, and most importantly EATING vegan food, I enjoy many other activities which include tap dancing, yoga, running a radio show on my school’s radio station, watching sitcoms ( I can’t believe Parks & Rec is in its last season) , and hanging out with my younger cousins.

Janki Vansia
