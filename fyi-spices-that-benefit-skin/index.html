FYI: Skin Health Benefits from Spices

Let food be your makeup

On my instagram, @hungrygreenhippo, I introduced a tea that I make daily. It is simply hot water with 3 spices added to it. These spices are: turmeric, coriander, and cumin!This tea is a lifesaver. It has kept my acne down, and for that, I am thankful. I literally do not have to wear cover-up anymore or try to hide my red acne spots with hair. These three spices are highly beneficial, and here is why

  1. Turmeric: Turmeric helps fight acne because of its antiseptic and antibacterial properties. It is common in the Indian culture to rub turmeric paste on one’s skin to get rid of discoloration, acne, and to make one’s overall complexion glow. Turmeric is also helpful in the reduction of wrinkles and dark circles! Also, from personal experience, turmeric mixed with lemon makes a GREAT exfoliator. If you want to know more about this mask, please comment, and I can make a tutorial! :)
  2.  Coriander: Coriander is known to help fight problems such as eczema, rashes, and inflammation.
  3. Cumin:  Cumin helps fight aging because it contains high amounts of Vitamin E. If you suffer from itchy, burning skin then a glass of water with cumin mixed in it helps reduce this burning and itching sensation. 

Who needs expensive skin care treatments?
