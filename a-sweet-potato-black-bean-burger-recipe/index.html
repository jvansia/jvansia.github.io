A Sweet (Potato+ Black Bean) Burger Recipe

Swwet baby potatos

I used to pay $12 for a vegan burger when I went out. Now, I can make it at home for a much cheaper price. Eating healthy and affordable :) — just what my college self loves(needs!).

Ingredients :

-2 tsp of salt -1 tbsp of dry garlic powder -1 tsp of dry parsley -1 tsp of black pepper -1/4 tsp of cayenne pepper -1/4 tsp of dry ginger -1/4 tsp of dry cumin powder -1 tsp of dry coriander powder -16 oz of dry black beans -1 small onion -1 small red pepper -Sweet potatoes

Directions:

  1. Soak the dry black beans in water overnight
  2. Turn on the stove + Put the beans in a pot with 3 cups of water for an hour until it’s cooked
  3. Drain excess water out
  4. Take the beans and add the ingredients listed in green 
  5. Chop the onion + red pepper into little pieces
  6. Sauté the onion and pepper into a pan for 5 minutes
  7. SWEET POTATO TIME: Take how many sweet potatoes you want(I chose 2)
    1. Boil sweet potatoes in water for 15 minutes
    2. Peel skin
    3. Cut into tiny pieces
  8. Take the black beans and add the sautéd mixture and the sweet potatoes
  9. Mash the newly created mixture
  10. Make burger patties + cook on a pan
