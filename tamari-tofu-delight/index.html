Tamari Tofu Delight

This place has a good vibe

My friends always tell me that they would be vegan, but ‘it is too hard’. Becoming vegan, or maintaining a vegan lifestyle shouldn’t be seen as difficult, or time consuming.  I always strive for efficiency when I am cooking, and honestly finding out how to cook meals quick and easy is invigorating! This meal only took 15 minutes to make! :) It’s perfect for those who like Asian cuisine, and need a quick, healthy fix. Here is what you will need:

Ingredients:

  • Sprouted Tofu( I got mine from Trader Joe’s aka my go to store)
  • Broccoli
  • Gluten Free Tamari Sauce  (Most Tamari sauces have no wheat, so if you are _gluten free _check out this sauce)
  • Baby Bella Mushrooms
  • Sesame Oil
  • Sea Salt
  • Red Chili Paste

Directions:

1. Pan sear the tofu:

Take the cut tofu squares and pan sear them in sesame oil until each side is golden brown. Put these tofu squares aside on a plate.

Pan sear= Pan-searing uses high heat to seal in juices by forming a delicious crust. The key to pan-searing is making sure the pan is hot enough. To test a pan for readiness, sprinkle a drop of water onto the pan; the water should sizzle or jump around. (Cooking School: How to Pan Sear, Martha Stewart)

2.Cook the vegetables: 

Take a pan, place it on the stove, and add red chili paste & sesame oil. Then let that cook until the chili paste starts to bubble. Then take cut vegetables (mushrooms and broccoli) and throw it in the pan.

~5 minutes later~

3.Add tofu to vegetable mixture:

Take the tofu that was just cooked, and add it to the vegetable mixture which is still cooking. Mix the mixture together. Then add 4 tablespoonfuls of Tamari sauce. _The reason why you add the Tamari sauce last is because you want to retain flavor, and not have evaporate because of the high heat. _Finish it off with sea salt.
